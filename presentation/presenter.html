What Is Security?

The “CIA triad”:

  • Confidentiality
  • Integrity
  • Availability

Confidentiality

Only view data you’re authorized to view.

Integrity

When you request / submit data, it’s the “right” data.

Availability

Your data is there when you want it.

Our focus: Integrity

Prevent players from tampering with data.

Protecting Data In Transit

TLS / SSL

  • The s in https://
  • Encryption prevents eavesdropping
  • Checksums prevent tampering
  • Inexpensive (~$10 / year via Namecheap)

Roll Your Own

Prevent tampering.

  • Send data in plaintext
  • Calculate hash of data + secret key
  • On server, recalculate hash and compare

Roll Your Own: Caveats

  • Assumes confidentiality is unimportant
  • Relies on impossible (though possibly manageable) task of keeping secret key safe
See Blu-ray AACS encryption key controversy for futility of hiding a secret key.
